OpenVPN Server

02/06/2018

This Monday we got our SSL VPN on x2. We setup OpenVPN Access Server on top of CentOS 7 and in Untangle. OpenVPN AS is a super friendly way to VPN into your house while out and about. From Android to iOS and Mac to Windows, OpenVPN has a client for you with plenty of third party options as well.
